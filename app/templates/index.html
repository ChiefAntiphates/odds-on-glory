How to play:

  1. Create an account and login
  2. If you want, purchase a gladiator from the Market who will fight on your behalf
  3. In the Colosseum, either host a new arena or join an ongoing one
  4. If you want, enter your gladiator into combat! If they win you win a large cash sum
  5. Place your bets! Each gladiator has different odds that will change throughout the arena, place your bets on any gladiator and hope they pay off
  6. To give the gladiator your backing an advantage, you can purchase medicine or traps to send to them in the arena
  7. Check your profile each hour to claim your bonus gold
  8. Check back to the home screen to see where you are on the leaderboard!
